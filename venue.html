Conference venue

Location

The symposium will take place in Mecenas building in the Faculty of Sciences .

How to arrive

From the airport: Federico García Lorca Granada-Jaén airport is about 21 km from Granada. There is a taxi stop in the entrance of the airport. It is also possible to take a bus: http://www.autocaresjosegonzalez.com , which leaves to the city for each arrival.

From the train station: Train station is at Avda. Andaluces

From the bus station: Bus station is at Avda. Juan Pablo II.
From the bus stop at the entrance of the bus station, take bus number 10 to "Camino de Ronda 8" stop, close to Faculty of Sciences. To go to the city center take either bus number 3 or 33.

At the main entrance of both, train and bus station, there is a taxi stop and you can contact them calling to +34 958 280 654. For further information on the buses, please click here.
